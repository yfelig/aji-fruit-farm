Privacy

What we collect, and what we don't

Aji Fruit Farm is a small family-run guesthouse in Embilipitiya, Sri Lanka. This website exists to help guests find us, learn about the farm, and reach us on WhatsApp. We do our best to keep things simple — including how we handle information about you.

Analytics

We use Cloudflare Web Analytics to understand which pages visitors look at and where they're coming from. It is privacy-first by design:

  • No cookies are set on your device.
  • No data is stored in your browser (no localStorage, no fingerprinting).
  • IP addresses are not stored in full.
  • No cross-site tracking or advertising profiles.

Because nothing personal is collected or stored, you don't need to give consent for us to count a page view.

WhatsApp

If you tap any of the WhatsApp buttons on this site, you'll be taken to WhatsApp and may exchange messages with Aji directly. Those messages are governed by WhatsApp's own privacy policy, not ours.

Hosting

The site is hosted on GitHub Pages. Standard server logs (IP, user-agent, requested file) may be kept by GitHub for security and abuse prevention, per GitHub's privacy statement. We don't read or use these logs ourselves.

Booking

We don't take bookings or payments through this website. Bookings happen on third-party platforms (Airbnb, Booking.com, TripAdvisor, Agoda) or directly with Aji on WhatsApp.
